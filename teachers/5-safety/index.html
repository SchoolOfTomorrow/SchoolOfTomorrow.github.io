Is it safe to participate?

There is no known harm associated with the tests and procedures implemented in our study. Study participation can be terminated at any time without any disadvantages for you or your pupils. Data will be treated in accordance with Swiss data protection laws. Data will be anonymized to report study results. Personal data will never be published that individuals can be identified. The data will never be sold. The study has been approved by the local ethics committee of Swiss Distance University Institute (ethics application #2019-12-00002).

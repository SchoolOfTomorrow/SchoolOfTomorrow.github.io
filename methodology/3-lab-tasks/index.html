The laboratory tasks

Those tests are provided in our laboratory. They intend to test the performance at perceving semantic differences and at translating vocabulary of a foreign language.

Fastball test

This test measures the performance at differentiating the semantic categories of images at the cognitive level. It uses the fastabll paradigm. For example, five images are presented in a sequence, four images of birds followed by a fifth image of a fruit. The cognitive reaction is assessed by comparing the reaction to the first four images belonging to the same semantic category versus the fifth image belonging to a different semantic category. A different reaction is expected for someone differenciating the semantic categories.

Cross-language priming test

This test measures the performance at translating words at the behavioral and cognitive levels. The participant has to decide if a translation is correct or false, and to tell if the answer was guessed or known. The behavior is assessed regarding the number of correct answers. The cognitive reaction is assessed by comparing the reaction to correct translations versus false translations. A different reaction is expected for someone differenciating the correct and false translations.

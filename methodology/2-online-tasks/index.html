The online tasks

Those tests are provided in a classroom setting. They intend to test sustained attention and fluid reasoning, which are essential for successful learning. Both tests are non-verbal, which make them particularly adapted for children aged between 10 and 12, ages at which the differences in language can still be broad.

Sustained attention test

If you want to try this test, click here!

Attention is a multimodal concept that resorts to several processes: sensory selection, response selection, attentional capacity and sustained performance. Attention is commonly described by models including five components: alertness, focused attention, selective attention, divided attention and sustained attention.

This test measures the ability at reacting when the target (either a square or a smurf) is at the top of the screen, and at non-reacting when it is at the bottom of it.

Vocabulary test

After using the app for a while, we want to measure how much vocabulary was learned. This test will be conducted by students from classes that use the app and students from classes that did not use the app. The content of this test we be based on the vocabulary.
